MovieSense: Empowering Movie Reviews with AI

Oct 2023 (Individual project)

In this project, I aimed to practice and deepen my understanding of NLP (Natural Language Processing) techniques. MovieSense is an advanced NLP-driven tool designed to analyze the sentiments of movie reviews. It offers concise summaries and facilitates understanding across languages by translating reviews from English to French. Utilizing cutting-edge algorithms and models, MovieSense is crafted to enrich usersâ€™ engagement with movie critiques.

Key Features:
  • Sentiment Analysis: Employs the Naive Bayes Classifier to determine whether movie reviews are positive or negative, using datasets from Rotten Tomatoes.
  • Dynamic Summarisation: Incorporates Facebook's BART model to distill lengthy reviews into short, comprehensible summaries.
  • Translation: Enables a broader audience reach by seamlessly translating reviews from English to French using the Helsinki-NLP model.
